# Roles and Responsibilities

## User

The user is the main person who uses the Nutrition Assistant application.

### Registration and Profile Management

* Create an account using personal details such as name, email, age, weight, height, gender, and activity level.
* Update profile information whenever required.

### Food Tracking

* Add food items consumed during the day.
* Enter the quantity or serving size of food items.
* Maintain a daily food consumption record.

### Diet Plan Management

* Create diet plans based on personal goals and preferences.
* View active diet plans and monitor progress.
* Modify diet plans when necessary.

### Viewing Nutritional Information

* Check calories, proteins, carbohydrates, and fats for food items and meals.
* Review nutritional information to make better food choices.

### Following Recommendations

* View meal suggestions and diet recommendations provided by the application.
* Use the suggestions to maintain a balanced diet.

### Progress Tracking

* Monitor daily and weekly nutrition intake.
* View reports and progress towards health goals.

---

## System

The system is responsible for processing user requests and providing nutrition-related information.

### System Responsibilities

* Store user information securely.
* Calculate BMI and nutritional values.
* Generate meal recommendations.
* Manage diet plans and food records.
* Generate reports and progress summaries.
* Provide nutritional information for food items and meals.
